# TaqwaPath

TaqwaPath is a calm, modern Islamic utility for daily worship, Islamic learning, and reflection.

It brings Salah timings, Quran recitations, Hadith collections, Zakat calculation, Hijri dates, Islamic calendar pages, and Ramadan references into one fast, clean, mobile friendly experience.

Website: https://taqwapath.com

Android app: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.taqwapath.taqwapath_mobile

Repository: https://github.com/talhazahid/taqwapath

Follow URL: https://github.com/talhazahid/taqwapath

## What Is TaqwaPath?

TaqwaPath is built for Muslims who want a simple place to access daily Islamic essentials without jumping between multiple websites or apps.

The goal is not only to provide Islamic information, but to make it easier to use. The interface is designed to feel calm, readable, fast, and respectful. Users can check prayer times, listen to Quran recitations, search Hadith collections, calculate Zakat, and follow important Islamic dates from one place.

## Why TaqwaPath Exists

Many Islamic websites are useful but feel cluttered, slow, or difficult to navigate on mobile. TaqwaPath is built with a UX first approach.

The product focuses on clear page titles, simple navigation, fast loading pages, mobile friendly layouts, calm visual design, search friendly content, internal linking between Islamic topics, and helpful summaries for users, search engines, and AI assistants.

TaqwaPath is designed to help people find the right Islamic tool quickly, understand what they are seeing, and continue their worship or learning without distraction.

## Zakat Calculator

The Zakat calculator helps users estimate Zakat due on eligible assets.

| Asset type | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| Cash | Cash in hand, bank balance, savings |
| Receivables | Loans given out |
| Investments | Investments and shares |
| Gold and silver | Gold weight and silver weight |
| Trade goods | Stock intended for sale |
| Liabilities | Credit purchases, wages due, rent, utilities, taxes, and other liabilities |

The calculator shows total assets, total liabilities, net zakatable wealth, nisab threshold, and estimated Zakat due at 2.5 percent.

It also includes a hawl reminder so users can confirm whether they have owned the wealth for one lunar year.

Important note: The calculator is provided for convenience and educational use. Zakat rulings can vary by scholar, school of thought, asset type, and personal situation, so users should consult a qualified scholar when needed.

## Content And Accuracy Notes

TaqwaPath provides Islamic tools and references for convenience.

Prayer times may vary by calculation method, madhab, location, and local authority. Hijri and Islamic calendar dates may shift based on moon sighting. Zakat calculations are estimates and should not replace advice from a qualified Islamic scholar.

## Design Goal

The design goal of TaqwaPath is to make Islamic daily tools feel easier, calmer, and more accessible.

Instead of overwhelming the user with heavy layouts, the interface keeps the focus on practical questions:

- What time is the next prayer?
- Which surah do I want to listen to?
- Which Hadith collection do I want to browse?
- How much Zakat may be due?
- What is the upcoming Islamic date?
