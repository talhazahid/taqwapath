# TaqwaPath

TaqwaPath is an Islamic lifestyle, worship, and knowledge platform built with Astro. It brings together Islamic calendar pages, Hijri date references, zakat calculation, hadith collections, Quran resources, and prayer time pages in one fast, searchable website.

Follow URL: https://github.com/talhazahid/taqwapath

## What TaqwaPath Offers

### Islamic Calendar and Hijri Date Pages

TaqwaPath includes dedicated Islamic calendar pages for Gregorian years with Hijri dates, Islamic months, and important Muslim events. The calendar experience helps users compare Gregorian dates with Hijri dates for planning Ramadan, Eid ul Fitr, Eid ul Adha, Muharram, Ashura, Hajj, Arafah, Laylat al Qadr, Shab e Barat, and other important Islamic days.

SEO focus: Islamic calendar, Hijri calendar, today Hijri date, Islamic date today, Gregorian to Hijri calendar, Muslim calendar, Ramadan calendar, Eid dates, Muharram date, Ashura date, Islamic holidays.

### Zakat Calculator

The zakat calculator helps Muslims estimate zakat due on cash, savings, loans given, investments, gold, silver, trade goods, and deductible liabilities. It uses nisab thresholds based on gold and silver and updates totals live as users enter values. The calculator supports international currencies and updates displayed gold and silver rates when a different currency is selected.

SEO focus: zakat calculator, calculate zakat online, zakat on gold, zakat on silver, zakat on cash, nisab calculator, Islamic finance calculator, zakat due, 2.5 percent zakat, Muslim charity calculator.

### Hadith Collection

TaqwaPath provides a structured hadith collection with searchable hadith data, book pages, chapter pages, and share-friendly hadith references. The hadith section is designed for fast reading, internal linking, and easy discovery of Islamic knowledge from recognized hadith collections.

SEO focus: hadith collection, Sahih Bukhari hadith, Islamic hadith, hadith search, hadith chapters, Prophet Muhammad hadith, Islamic knowledge, authentic hadith, daily hadith.

### Prayer Times and Salah Timings

TaqwaPath includes prayer time pages for salah and namaz timings by country and city. Users can find daily Muslim prayer times for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha, with location-focused pages designed for local search discovery.

SEO focus: prayer times, salah times, namaz timings, Muslim prayer schedule, Fajr time, Dhuhr time, Asr time, Maghrib time, Isha time, Islamic prayer times by city.

### Quran Resources

The platform includes Quran-focused pages and resources for users looking to read, listen, and explore Quran content online. These pages support the broader Islamic knowledge hub and connect naturally with prayer, hadith, calendar, and zakat tools.

SEO focus: Quran online, read Quran online, listen Quran, Quran recitation, Islamic Quran resources, Muslim Quran reading.

## Technical Overview

TaqwaPath is built with Astro and plain CSS, without Tailwind. The project focuses on fast static pages, clean semantic markup, reusable Astro components, and SEO-friendly content structures.

Core scripts:

- `npm run dev` starts the local Astro development server.
- `npm run build` builds the production site.
- `npm run preview` previews the production build.
- `npm run scrape:sunnah` runs the Sunnah data scraper.

## Project Goals

TaqwaPath aims to be a practical Islamic web platform for Muslims searching for reliable worship tools and Islamic references. The site is structured around high-intent search needs such as Islamic calendar dates, zakat calculation, prayer times, Quran reading, and hadith browsing.
